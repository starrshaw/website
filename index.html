About The Work

I'm a Design Technologist and Technical Artist who thrives at the intersection of art, programming, and emerging technologies, bringing ideas to life through a blend of creative vision and technical execution.

With a strong foundation in both traditional art and modern digital pipelines, I've built a career spanning 3D technical art, game development, and design-driven innovation. At Amazon and Amazon Web Services (AWS), I've contributed across multiple roles, from 3D Artist to Lead 3D Technical Artist, and now Design Technologist, where I focus on prototyping and creating next-generation AI-driven experiences. My work bridges interaction design, real-time systems, and AI to explore more human-centered, autonomous, and immersive interfaces.

I bring a unique combination of hands-on artistic skills, including traditional painting, drawing, and sculpting, and deep technical expertise in tools, pipelines, and programming. This dual perspective allows me to design and build solutions that are both visually compelling and technically robust, whether that's optimizing 3D workflows, developing custom tools, or experimenting with generative AI and real-time systems.

Throughout my career, I've worked across industries including games, interactive media, product design, and AR/VR, contributing to projects ranging from game art and tools development to early-stage concept visualization and AI experimentation.

I'm driven by curiosity and a constant desire to learn, create, and innovate. Whether I'm developing new pipelines, exploring generative AI, or creating art, I'm always pushing to stay ahead of where technology and creativity are going, and to help shape what comes next.
